# Code of Conduct

TraceDB community spaces are for focused technical collaboration.

Be respectful, stay on topic, and assume public maintainers are working from
limited context. Do not post secrets, private customer data, credentials,
private endpoints, or hosted-service incident details in public repositories.

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions that are
abusive, spammy, unrelated to the public distribution, or unsafe to keep public.
